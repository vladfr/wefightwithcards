Kobolds and Catacombs is the latest Hearthstone expansion, launched on December 7. It features a sleuth of new cards that are bound to shake the meta.

Among them are the new Legendary weapons, powerful one-of's that promise to bring back weapon-hate cards, and Spellstones, cards which get free upgrades as the match progresses.

But I thought I'd take a look at the new Ladder staples: cards that you'll see time and again on the Ladder. You know, like Bonemare.

We'll exclude Legendaries, since they're a one-off, and let's face it — you know that list will be just {{< card "Patches" "hs" >}}.

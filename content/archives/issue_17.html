SMITE Tactics on consoles
//techraptor
Smite Hand of the Gods is an upcoming card-game launching in Alpha on consoles. People interested in the game can register for the Closed Alpha of SMITE Tactics for PlayStation 4 and Xbox One.


Legendary DXP - digital deck-building game
//toucharcade
Upper Deck, the creator of physical deck-building games based on the Marvel & DC universes is launching a digital version on August 17 on consoles and on PC/Steam.

There will be a few different modes of play. Solo play pits you against the AI, or you can take on a friend or unknown human opponent in a quick or customisedkick match. There is also the Gauntlet, a five-player tournament where you'll battle it out for in-game rewards.

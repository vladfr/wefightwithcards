#discover

Krosmaga
Steam/PC+Mac / iOS / Android

A lane-based tactical collectible card game set in the universe of Dofus / Wakfu. This game is reminiscent of the lane-based card game Scrolls from Mojang (Microsoft) and is extremely well polished with great gameplay and strategy. It's Free-to-Play, but you can spend money as well. All content is available through daily quests and a limited arena mode with a guaranteed card of the highest rarity for going undefeated. It also has an interesting twist for CCGs: your highest rarity cards gain experience and level up as you play with them, which often keeps me excited to play just one more.

The release of Krosmaga's first expansion saw not just new cards but entirely new ways to win the game. More new cards will be introduced soon in a Halloween pack and there are plans to create an official e-sports circuit. This is my current favorite lesser known CCG and I think it has amazing potential. It's truly a blast to play casually, even without great e-sports support.

Today's guest editor is Ryan "Leo" Elworth / reach out to him at CHILLEO@GMAIL.COM
 

Underhand
iOS AppStore / Android / FREE
I loved this little Cultist Card Game for its Lovecraftian feel and solo play experience. You're building your own cult, with the sole purpose of summoning an ancient demon. You need to respond to event cards and manage your resources in order to complete rituals. You can recruit cultists, read old books, trade food, rob banks, and do it all like a boss while fooling the police. 

#news

Duelyst: Immortal Vanguards adds over 100 new cards and new mechanics to the popular CCG. It's out on November 14 for PC on Steam.

Stem: Epic Heroes is a new physical game for kids. It's a 2-4 player game with famous scientists playing the role of super-heroes who help you discover and learn science. It launched on Kickstarter and was an instant hit, and looks like a great gift.

Doomtrooper is an old paper CCG getting a new life as a digital version in another successful Kickstarter. It's legitimate cyber-punk alien warfare with cards, so if that strikes your fancy, hurry up because the campaign ends tomorrow morning: Kickstarter page here.

About

Hello! I'm Vlad, nice to meet you!

I'm a software developer and consultant. I play Hearthstone regularly, but I wanted to get into other card games as well. I thought of doing this newsletter to discover a new game with a good community and keep up to date.

What kind of content will I get?

I plan to include the most relevant links & updates from the community. These can be reddit posts, blog posts, developer updates, state of the meta etc.

I selected 2 games. What will I see?

There will be a General section with news from across the industry. Then, each game will have its own Section. You'll only receive the sections you signed up for.

I'll also include snapshots of other games being added, so you can track more games if you want.

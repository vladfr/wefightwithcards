MTG Arena beta timeline

Upcoming October streams, a closed beta in November and ambitious plans for 2018

MTG: Arena is a new digital CCG in Wizard's Magic: the Gathering family. We're covering all the latest updates in our Arena series.

Jump to: Cards - Beta info - Timeline - FAQs

  • Dev streams in October on Twitch
  • Closed NDA beta starts in November
  • Streaming allowed early 2018
  • PC Only for now
  • Launches in 2018

The MTG: Arena team is gearing up for the Closed Beta with a series of streams in which they talk more about the game. There's great gameplay videos as well as pre-alpha footage, some even from before the art team got a chance to work their magic.

It's a game very much under development, but the team has done great work so far and it's a rewarding experience to follow along with their progress.

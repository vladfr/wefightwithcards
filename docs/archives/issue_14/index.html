Discover

Bushido Breaker: A Hidden Movement & Deduction Card Game
//physical card game

This is a new Kickstarted card game for 2-4 players. It has some unique mechanics: you're either a ninja or a samurai, and you move silently: you write down actions, select cards, and they resolve simultaneously at the end.

The reviews are very favourable. One reviewer concluded: "it creates an atmosphere so darn intense I can’t play it two or more times in a single sitting".

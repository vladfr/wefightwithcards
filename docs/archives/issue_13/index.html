Discover

HEX: Shards of Fate
//Win, Mac
Hex is a RPG-style card battler, with a full-fledged PvE mode, Auction house, customisable heroes, card enhancements & more. They just released a new expansion called Frostheart with new cards, heroes, and stories.


Solitairica: Primal Champions
//iOS and Android
The class-based solo card game received it's new expansion recently, and it's well worth the look. At $1 it's a no-brainer for those long commutes. Here's the original trailer.


South Park: Phone Destroyer preview
//mobile
A card battler with Kenny & Cartman? Pocketgamer does a preview of this upcoming mobile game. You send characters into battle by playing cards, then activate powers and watch them fight it out!

The battles are like Clash Royale, so it all happens in real-time. There is a story mode, and a multi-player component.


Rival Books of Aster
//iOS, PC/Steam
New to the Steam early access list, Aster is a strategy-first CCG where card placement and combos are key. It's easy to pick up, but hard to master. You build your deck, and fight to uncover the secrets of the Books in a living story. There's also a PvP ladder and weekly events. Check it out on Steam!

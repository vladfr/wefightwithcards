#discover

Magic: the Gathering Arena
Wizards announced that they're working on a new MtG digital game called Magic: the Gathering Arena. We don't know much else about it except that more info will be released in early September, including a game demo. Can't wait!

Miracle Merchant
iOS Universal and Android
From the makers of Card Thief comes yet another great solitaire card game. "If you like one-player digital card games you owe it to yourself to check this one out." - PocketTactics

Pathfinder Duels
This is an upcoming digital CCG in the popular Pathfinder universe. No word on a release date yet, but there's something really cool about this one: it will feature simultaneous turns! Whatever that means, we'll keep you informed.

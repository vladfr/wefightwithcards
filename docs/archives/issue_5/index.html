Paladin: In UnGoro, anyfin can happen

Paladin grows in popularity, and is now considered a tier 1 deck. We saw lots of murlocs in #HGG this week, and you're more and more likely to bump into them on ladder. Let's see what Paladins have to say in Un'Goro!

Budget handbuff
//standard

//wild
If murlocs are not your thing, this crazy fun deck profits off of a hoard of silver hand recruits. Sunkeeper Tarim makes his presence known, and we pay a visit to our old friend Kel'Thuzad. If you don't have them, Stegodons are always fun.

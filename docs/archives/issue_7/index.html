Class Check: Shaman

Shaman dominated the previous meta, with both Aggro and Midrange/Jade versions really tearing up the ladder. After the Spirit Claws nerf, Aggro took a step back. Now, Un'Goro has Elementals, and mid-Shaman is back.

The Shammy is a good beginner class - here's a basic, zero-dust Bloodlust deck to get you started. This deck is really good, easy to pick up, and can pull fast wins. After you grow your collection, you can start adding the Elemental and/or Jade packages.

Evolve Shaman is another deck type that has seen play.

And I'd be remiss not to mention Firebat's latest Shaman Deck Doctors:
#2 - White Eyes  and #3 - Elemental Shaman
This series continues to be really good, and if you want to play Shaman, these deck doctors will give you some great insight into the class and two quality mid-range decks to play.

Gwent Public Beta is coming tomorrow on PC, Xbox and PS4. Last week saw a ton of news about the upcoming changes to the game, so let's dig in:
  • the new player experience is vastly improved with Challenges and Starter decks
  • Challenges are solo play, last for 5 hours and will net rewards
  • you can get all faction leaders from Challenges
  • Starter decks have 6 Silver cards and 4 Gold cards, which is excellent

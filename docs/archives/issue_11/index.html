Discover

Astro Boy: Edge of time
//PC
Astro Boy, the famous manga character, now has an online TCG, and it's free! The game has a fresh design, inspired from the mangas, and has both a story mode and online multiplayer.

The gameplay isn't unique, but it's quite fun: you and your opponent have two lanes each, one for offensive creatures - Enforcers, one for defensive stuff - Guardians. The game is only available on Windows, although it looks promising.


Cards and Castles
iOS / Android / Steam
This is a fun game which basically adds a board & unit positioning to Hearthstone's simple but addictive gameplay. You can play solo and multi-player, and the game is updated and balanced often enough to provide quality play.


Helionox
//physical
Another Kickstarter game that's already made it past it's goal, with 7 days to go. Helionox is a deck-building movement game for 1 to 4 players, with both coop and competitive game modes. It features an in-game card market where you buy cards with credits won during the game. 

Oaxaca: Crafts of a Culture
//physical
In beautiful Oaxaca, Mexico, crafts are the town's blood. Play alone or compete with up to 3 other players to create the most beautiful works of art and sell them for points. Whoever sells the most wins. The campaign is almost done on Kickstarter, and from their page, the game itself looks like a work of art.

Ducklings - parenting card game
//tabletopgaming
This is something new - a cooperative + competitive card game, with ducks no less, aimed at teaching children on how to make choices and learn what parenting is about. Here's the trailer + intro - features real ducks! Which is good, since this game is very emotional and brings some really tough choices to the players.

Hearthstone

This week saw some greatly anticipated news: Hearthstone packs will give unique cards first, including legendaries. This will start in the next expansion.

A League for Hearthstone Players
//dadlegend
A fresh perspective on competitive play outside of official tournaments, @YellowDart3 tries Team Heath League and brings us an inside view of this pro amateur league. There's teams, rosters, structured play and etiquette. If that's your thing, head on over to teamhearthleague.com to see what the fuss is all about.

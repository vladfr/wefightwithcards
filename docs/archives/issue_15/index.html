Discover

Shadow Era
Mac/PC/mobile

Shadow Era is already established as a solid trading card game with over 4 million players and over 500 cards.

But this week saw the launch of an unique idea: you'll be able to buy shares in the game and trade them on a decentralised market.

That's right! People can buy Shadow Tokens with PayPal or Bitcoin. Tokens give you the right to share a monthly profit with the game developers, and you can also sell them on an open blockchain market. Of course, you'll also get some sweet in-game rewards.

Head over to shadowtoken.io - the sale starts on August 1st.


StormBound
Android
A CCG + RTS mix just soft-launched on the Play market. Made by Kongregate, the once browser-gaming platform, it's available on Play but just on some regions, with more to come.

#discover

Stormbound
// iOS / Android

Another mobile card game that blends cards with unit-placement. You create a deck from 12 unique cards, and you'll draw up to 4 of these cards each turn. Cards are either units, castles or spells, and they go onto a 4-lane board where they fight it out in hand-to-hand combat! The goal is for your troops to reach the enemy base and deal 10 points of damage.
 

Minion Masters
Steam / PC

This is an Early Access game that's basically Dota with cards. It's 90% off on Steam right now, so it's a good grab. The game should be released in January 2018, and will feature 1v1 or 2v2 battles, and Twitch tournaments.

#discover


MtG: Arena revealed

Magic: the Gathering is finally taking a modern shape with the upcoming MtG: Arena. You can sign up for the closed beta on PlayMTGArena.com

Arena will be focused on the latest Standard sets, including all cards from the current rotation, and will only feature constructed play at first. They plan to expand it later to include Limited play (i.e. Hearthstone Arena). The critics are mostly positive so far, but the game is far from finished. Everybody agrees on one thing though: it feels like Magic.
 

Card City Nights 2 launched on Steam, and they launched a patch on Friday with detailed rules and achievements. Get it while it's hot!
 

HEX: Card Clash on PS4
Hex Entertainment's trading card game, Hex: Card Clash, is coming to PlayStation 4 with up to $5000 in cash prizes, and cross-play with the PC version, Hex: Shards of Fate.
 

Top Drives: CCG with cars
Android / iOS
This is a neatly unique game which combines card games with racing. Available on mobile, the game is doing really well, reaching 1 million downloads in it's first week on Android.

#gwent

Major changes to Gwent recently: Gold cards are now targetable and get effects. Gold cards were very different from other games, in that most effects ignored them. This is a big change, nerfing many good cards and providing new opportunities for others.

Merchant looks at the most important Gold cards in this new light.

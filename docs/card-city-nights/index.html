All the updates on the anticipated game & open beta keys!

It's always night in space

In the flying city-starship of Frivolity, all the residents are obsessed with the latest razzle-dazzle: The Card Game. Battle with the denizens to take their cards, while exploring the city to discover it's mysteries.

The game is played by two players on a single board. You play your cards in the hopes of linking them together to build combos. These combos can then be activated to deal damage, heal, shield or gain other kooky effects.

Card City Nights 2

Apart from the long-awaited multiplayer, the new game features over 200 new cards with all your favourite heroes and villains, plus a reworked game board and balance changes. Beta #1 had 100 players and helped squash a lot of bugs and resolve issues; so this next beta will hopefully be much smoother and an enjoyable experience.

The game's official launch date is September 1st on Steam. Beta #2 starts June 23 until July 10. There will be over 1000 players in the game, battling it out for cards and glory. Subscribe to get your key!

And remember, it’s always night in space.
